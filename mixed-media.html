A Note on Intros

It is preferred that you attribute Sagittarius at game startup using the video above. However, if this is not possible because:
  • Your game does not support videos: Center the Sagittarius logo on either a white or #E7E7E7 background as a splash screen, optionally with fade-in and fade-out
  • Your game has no splash screens: Place either the Sagittarius logo or icon on a credits screen where appropriate
